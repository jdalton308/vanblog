Van Build: Online Resources

I wish I could say that I had the creativity and foresight to build my van without any inspiration, but that's just not so. If you landed here, then you are probably also aware of 10 other sites that are also trying to help you out. But, if you aren't aware of those sites, or videos, or forums, then let me introduce you:

Websites

  • Van Dog Dweller

    The best resource I found, especially for electrical details. The van/bus looks beautiful, and he also posts regularly about his continuing adventures.

  • Build a Green RV

    A fellow Promaster conversion, but much he did a much more thorough job documenting the steps than I was able to. A great resource for everything from insulation to electrical, though his build is more elaborate than mine here.

  • DIY RV

    A large site devoted to projects around RVs and campervans, with a few guides as well.

  • Some Aussie's Sprinter

    An article posted on DIY RV (just above), a cool build of a Sprinter.

  • Mike's Van

    I don't know Mike, but here's a post on his blog about updates he made from his first build. It's cool to see what he wanted to change after spending some time in the van.

  • Tiny Camper

    Another van-conversion blog.
